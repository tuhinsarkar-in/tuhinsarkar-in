    Privacy Policy

    Last updated: August 15, 2025

    Welcome to Tuhin Sarkar's official website. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit my website. I am committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on my site.

    Information I Collect

    Personal Information

    I may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to me when you:

    • Contact me through my contact form
    • Leave comments or feedback

    This information may include:

    • Name and email address
    • Message content
    • Any other information you choose to provide

    Automatically Collected Information

    When you visit my website, I automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

    • IP address and location data
    • Browser type and version
    • Operating system
    • Pages visited and time spent on pages
    • Referring website
    • Device information

    How I Use Your Information

    I use the information I collect to:

    • Provide and maintain my website
    • Respond to your inquiries and provide support
    • Analyze website usage and improve user experience
    • Comply with legal obligations
    • Protect against fraud and abuse

    Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    I use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. For detailed information about my use of cookies, please see my Cookie Policy.

    Types of Cookies I Use

    • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality
    • Analytics Cookies: Help me understand how visitors use my site
    • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

    Third-Party Services

    I may use third-party services that collect, monitor, and analyze data, including:

    • Google Analytics: Website analytics and performance monitoring
    • GoatCounter: Privacy-friendly website analytics

    These third-party services have their own privacy policies, and I encourage you to review them.

    Data Retention

    I retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

    Data Security

    I implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

    Your Rights

    Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

    • Access: Request access to your personal information
    • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
    • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
    • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
    • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information
    • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent
